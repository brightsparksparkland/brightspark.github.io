About The school Bus

In an effort to live up to our motto "success through effort", Arya, a public Primary School has made real effort to acquire a school bus. This has been made possible through contributions and donations of all stakeholders i.e parents, pupils, teachers, donors, DEB etc.
This has enabled us to be the only public primary school in this region to own a school bus. However, the repayment of the bus loan is far from over and we are thus still requesting for well wishes, old boys, donors etc to generously contribute so we can finally put paid to the project. Thanking you in advance.
