About Us

Arya Primary school is a public mixed primary school in Kisumu county Kenya , situated along Jommo Kenyatta highway oposite United mall, the school was founded by the Arya Samaj community as a girls school in 1958 and was later converted to a mixed public school in 1968. On average it has over 2000 pupils down from ECD, middle to upper primary. Most classes are three streams each though it should be a five stream school.

Academic perfomance

In 2013 , 13 students scored over 400 marks out of 500 while the rest scored above 300 , in 2014 over 100 students scored above 300 marks

Outstading Features of the School

  • A fully equipped computer lab with 30 computers all accessible to pupils
  • Pioneers in E-learning in the region: have acquired e-content , laptop, projector and project digital content of the syllabus to the pupils
  • Fully equipped and functional science laboratory
  • Well stocked library
  • Numerous student clubs
  • The School also prides itself with a school Anthem
  • School bus
  • Organised School Trips and Tours
